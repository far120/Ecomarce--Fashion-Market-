Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Add products to your cart, then click the checkout button and follow the instructions to complete your order.

Can I change my order after placing it?

You can contact support to request changes if your order hasn't shipped yet.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash on delivery and online payments (credit/debit cards).

How can I track my order?

You can track your order status from your account dashboard after logging in.
